Welcome to my academic page! You probably know me as Xi-Ning Wang, Xining, or Ning. Others may or may not pronounce my name correctly, which is perfectly fine—for your convenience, "Ning" is simple and works well for me.

Recent News: I am moving back to Asia soon to start a new position.

Background: I completed my PhD in Education & Human-Computer Interaction (HCI) at Trinity College Dublin (School of Computer Science & Statistics and School of Education), Ireland.

Previously, I worked as a Research Fellow (Postdoc) at the NHS and the School of Medicine / School of Management at the University of St Andrews, UK. I was also a Guest Researcher at the Institute of Psychology, University of Copenhagen, Denmark.

My research and teaching expertise lies at the intersection of Psychology, Computer Science, and Education.
